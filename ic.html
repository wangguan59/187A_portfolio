After several group discussions, we decided to develop a mobile web store and a new POS system for our client, Invisible Children. I used Lucid chart to design a Ipad POS interface. In the POS system there are three major component: contact information, merchandise selection, and finalizing the order. The contact information is more like check-in process, we want the contact information no matter they are going to make a purchase or not. If they are new to the invisible children, and they don’t want make a purchase at this moment, they can still register for a newsletter, so they can receive updates of Invisible Children’s progress and activities.

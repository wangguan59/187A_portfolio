In the fall 2011, I ran several user experience tests for the ESL Genie. We invited some users to our office and let them use the website for 20 minutes. Then we had interviews about 30 minutes based on our observations and guideline questions. During the experiments, we found some interesting phenomenons, for example, some of them were confused about sign-in and sign-up field. Also, in the quiz section, not many people noticed that there was a explanation button.
